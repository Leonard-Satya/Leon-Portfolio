Paper Pulp Art

This art project features a 3D sunflower crafted entirely from handmade pulp, emphasizing texture and form. The use of pulp brings an organic, tactile quality to the piece, making the sunflower appear both delicate and lifelike.

Contemporary Art

This contemporary art project explores balance and repetition through a dynamic structure crafted entirely from wood sticks. By layering and interlocking the sticks, the piece creates a sense of movement and architectural rhythm, blending natural materials with geometric design.
